TERMS OF SERVICE

THE RULES OF THE ROAD

What you can expect from us. What we ask of you.

Last updated: May 24, 2026

1. Service Overview

Alamo Litter Patrol provides cat litter box swap, Litter-Robot cleaning, and scooping services in the San Antonio, TX area. Services are scheduled weekly or as agreed at sign-up.

2. Pricing & Billing

Pricing is published on our pricing page. We charge after your first completed visit; recurring service is billed weekly thereafter. Setup fees (where applicable) are charged once at sign-up.

3. Cancellation

You can cancel anytime by emailing hello@alamolitterpatrol.com. We'll stop billing after your current week's service. No cancellation fees.

4. Skipping a Week

Reply SKIP to our reminder text the day before service to skip that week. No charge for skipped weeks. If you don't respond, we'll deliver service as scheduled.

5. Access & Safety

For home-entry service, you agree to provide a safe path to the litter box and to confine pets that may be aggressive. For porch service, the box must be reasonably accessible. We reserve the right to skip a visit (and not charge) if access is unsafe or blocked.

6. Loaner Boxes

Box swap and Litter-Robot service involves a loaner box left at your home during sanitization. Loaner boxes remain our property and must be returned at cancellation. Damaged or missing loaners may be charged at replacement cost ($30–60).

7. Service Guarantee

If you're not satisfied with your first visit, contact us within 7 days for a full refund.

8. Liability

Alamo Litter Patrol carries general liability insurance. Our liability for any incident is limited to the cost of the affected service visit, except where prohibited by law.

9. Changes

We may update these terms with reasonable notice. Continued use of our service after updates constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

Questions about these terms? Email hello@alamolitterpatrol.com.
