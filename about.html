ABOUT US

BUILT BY CAT PEOPLE.
FOR CAT PEOPLE.

San Antonio's only dedicated cat litter service. Veteran-owned and operated, run out of Leon Valley.

Why we started

San Antonio has more than a dozen dog poop scooper services. We counted. Then we counted how many were dedicated to cat litter. Zero.

Cat people get the short end of the stick. Cat sitters bundle scooping into pricey visits stuffed with services nobody asked for. Generalist services don't know cats. And the day-to-day reality of a litter box — the smell, the dust, the daily ritual — just doesn't have to be your problem anymore.

So we built Alamo Litter Patrol. One job, done well. Weekly. On time. No fuss.

Our Promise

  • Show up on time. Same day, every week, unless otherwise communicated by you.
  • Respect your home. Porch service available — we never enter unless invited.
  • Treat your cat right. Quiet, fast swaps. Premium litter. Sanitized boxes.
  • Make it easy to leave. No contracts, no cancellation fees. If we're not earning it, you shouldn't have to fight to stop paying.
