PRIVACY POLICY

HOW WE HANDLE YOUR INFO

Short version: we only collect what we need to serve you. We don't sell your data.

Last updated: May 24, 2026

What we collect

When you sign up, we collect your name, address, email, phone number, ZIP, and details about your cat(s) and litter setup. We need all of it to deliver service.

How we use it

  • To schedule and deliver your service
  • To send appointment reminders, photo confirmations, and skip-week prompts
  • To process payment through our payment processor
  • To respond to your questions or feedback

How we store it

Customer info is stored in Google Sheets (form submissions) and our scheduling software. Payment info is handled by our payment processor (Stripe or Square) — we never see your full card number.

Who we share it with

Nobody, with these exceptions: our payment processor (to take payment), our scheduling software (to dispatch service), and any government or legal entity that requires it by law. We don't sell your data. We don't share it for marketing.

Your rights

You can ask us anytime to see what we have on file, correct it, or delete it. Email hello@alamolitterpatrol.com and we'll handle it within 7 days.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies — primarily for our intake form (Tally) and to remember settings. We don't run ad-tracking or third-party analytics that profile you.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hello@alamolitterpatrol.com.
