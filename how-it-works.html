HOW IT WORKS

IN THREE EASY STEPS

From sign-up to your first clean box, here's exactly what happens.

STEP 01

Pick Your Service

Choose what fits your home: box swap (we drop a clean pre-filled box, take the dirty one), Litter-Robot cleaning (we deep-clean your Robot at our facility and return it within 3 days), or scoop-only. Then pick home entry or porch service.

STEP 02

We Come Weekly

Same day, every week. We text you a heads-up the day before. Reply SKIP anytime to skip a week — no charge. On service day, we swap or clean in 2–3 minutes and send a photo confirmation.

STEP 03

Live Your Life

Let us take care of the scooping. No more lugging heavy litter. No more guilt. Cancel anytime — no contracts, no fees.

Pick The Service That Fits You

🟧 Box Swap

We bring a clean, pre-filled stainless steel box, take the dirty one, and you're done. Litter is included. The dirty box gets deep-cleaned and sanitized at our facility and returned the following week.

🤖 Litter-Robot Cleaning

If you own a Litter-Robot (or similar automatic box), we pick it up, deep-clean it at our facility, and return it within 3 calendar days. While yours is away, we leave a standard loaner box so your cat is never without.

🧹 Scoop Only / Dump + Refill

Don't need a swap? We'll come scoop your existing box. Or do a dump + refill where we remove everything and refill with fresh litter. Both require home entry. No setup fee.

🏠 Home Entry vs. Porch Service

Home entry is our standard for box swap and Litter-Robot service — we come inside, swap the box, leave. If your cat has porch access and you'd rather not have anyone come in, porch service knocks $15 off every visit. Scoop services always require home entry.

📱 Communication

The day before your service, you get a text. Need to skip a week, no problem, reply SKIP to skip that week with no charge.

🎖️ Senior Discount

10% off any service for customers 65+. Verified on first visit, no paperwork needed.

Fill out the form. We'll confirm your first service day within 24 hours.
